About San Antonio

Founded as a spanish mission and colonial outpost in 1731, San Antonio was incorporated in 1837. Known for being the home of the Alamo, its Botanical Garden, and the River Walk, San Antonio is a popular tourist destination in the heart of the Hill Country of Texas.

San Antonio is classified as an urban city, boasting a population of roughly 1.5 million. With a median household income of $59,994, San Antonio boasts a healthy economy in the military, health care, oil and gas, and tourism sectors, as well as a growing technology sector.
